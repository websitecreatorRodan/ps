PlayStation Consoles

PlayStation 1

The PlayStation 1 (PS1) is a home video game console developed and marketed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released in December 1994 and was the first of the PlayStation lineup of video game consoles.

PlayStation 2

The PlayStation 2 (PS2) is a home video game console developed and marketed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released in March 2000 and is the best-selling video game console of all time, having sold over 155 million units worldwide.

PlayStation 3

The PlayStation 3 (PS3) is a home video game console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released in November 2006 and introduced features like the PlayStation Network, multimedia capabilities, and a unified online gaming service.

PlayStation 4

The PlayStation 4 (PS4) is a home video game console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Released in November 2013, it competes with Microsoft's Xbox One and Nintendo's Wii U and Switch.

PlayStation 5

The PlayStation 5 (PS5) is the latest home video game console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Announced in 2019 and released in November 2020, it offers advanced graphics performance, a new controller with haptic feedback, and faster loading times.
